5-Step Strategic Action Plan

A practical roadmap for homestay owners to transform their properties into world-class accommodations.

1

Quality Assessment

Complete property audit, identify improvement areas, set baseline standards, and establish guest feedback systems.

2

Infrastructure Upgrade

Install reliable WiFi, upgrade bathrooms, improve bedding quality, and add modern conveniences while preserving charm.

3

Service Excellence

Train family members in hospitality, develop local experience packages, and create memorable cultural interactions.

4

Digital Presence

Professional photography, optimize OTA listings, build social media presence, and implement online booking systems.

5

Community Integration

Connect with local businesses, participate in regional marketing, and contribute to sustainable tourism development.
